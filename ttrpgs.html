World-Building & Campaign Architecture

Running complex tabletop campaigns is my ultimate exercise in collaborative storytelling. My design philosophy prioritizes player agency, atmospheric world-building, and deep, interconnected party dynamics.

I have extensive experience running and designing homebrew content for popular systems like Dungeons & Dragons 5e, Pathfinder 2e, and Call of Cthulhu. I specialize in weaving intricate narratives that adapt on the fly, creating custom rulesets and encounters that force players to think creatively rather than just relying on standard combat buffs.

Specialties: Homebrew Mechanics, Narrative Pacing, Encounter Balancing.
